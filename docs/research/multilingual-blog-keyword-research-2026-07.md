# Multilingual Blog Keyword Research Report

Project: ChinaFreeWeight / PowerBaseFit
Research date: 2026-07-18
Markets: International English, Brazil, Spain and Latin America

## Research method

The plan combines live search-result review, Google autocomplete sampling by language and country, competitor content-gap review, current-site topic inventory, and B2B buying-stage analysis. Existing indexed URLs, titles, canonicals, and article copy are excluded from the proposed URL set. The resulting ten topic clusters preserve valid hreflang relationships while each market receives its own keyword, buyer vocabulary, examples, structure, and search angle.

Observed demand patterns:

- English searches show strong competition around broad manufacturer and import guides. More specific RFQ, inspection, packaging, sample approval, and batch-control questions have clearer procurement intent and less generic competition.
- Brazilian demand uses practical commercial language such as `fabricante`, `fornecedor`, `atacado`, `academia`, `marca própria`, `importação`, `cotação` and `custo posto`. Buyers need Brazil-aware logistics language without unsupported tax-rate claims.
- Spanish demand spans Spain and Latin America. Neutral terms such as `proveedor`, `distribuidor`, `compra mayorista`, `equipos de gimnasio`, `marca propia`, `importación`, `cotización` and `control de calidad` travel better than country-specific slang.
- Competitive pages frequently make unverified capacity, savings, certification, and market-leadership claims. PowerBaseFit content should differentiate through specification discipline, sample approval, measurable QC, packaging, and explicit "confirm by model/order" boundaries.

## Topic-cluster and hreflang policy

Each numbered row forms one multilingual entity for hreflang. The three pages answer the same procurement problem, but they are not translations: headings, examples, buying terminology, cost cautions, checklists, FAQ wording, and CTA context are authored for each market. Every cluster will publish reciprocal `en`, `pt-BR`, `es`, and English `x-default` alternates only after all three URLs return 200 without redirects.

## Content safeguards

- No claims about certifications, customers, sales volume, market leadership, or fixed manufacturing capacity will be added without verified evidence.
- Duties, taxes, freight, lead time, MOQ, tolerances, warranty, and packaging specifications will be described as quote- or model-dependent.
- Existing English, Portuguese, and Spanish URLs and their customer-visible copy remain unchanged.
- New pages will link only to published pages in the same language.
- Customer-visible pages will not mention SEO, GEO, AI Search, keyword optimization, or ranking work.
